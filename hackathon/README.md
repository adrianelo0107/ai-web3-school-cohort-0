# Hackathon Workspace

This folder keeps hackathon thinking separate from daily notes.

## Workflow

1. Collect ideas in `ideas/`.
2. Convert promising ideas into a one-page product brief.
3. Define the smallest demoable workflow.
4. Track assumptions, risks, and user value.
5. Prepare README, pitch narrative, demo script, and submission checklist.

## Evaluation Questions

- Who has the problem?
- Why now?
- Why does AI improve the workflow?
- Why does Web3 matter beyond branding?
- What can be demoed in under 3 minutes?
- What is the biggest trust, security, or privacy risk?
